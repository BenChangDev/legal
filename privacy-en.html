2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain core features: image stylization, GIF/meme generation, result display and export.
  • Identity verification and anti-abuse: order verification, anti-fraud, risk control, and security audits.
  • Customer support and issue resolution: respond to your feedback and follow up.
  • Statistics and optimization: troubleshooting, performance, and experience improvements (preferably de-identified/aggregated).
  • If we add new processing purposes/scope, we will reasonably notify you and obtain your consent.

3. Legal Basis

We process information based on contract necessity, legal obligations, your consent, and public interest/major legitimate interests as applicable by law:

  • Using, storing, and delivering generated results; order verification: contract necessity.
  • Security and compliance reviews: legal obligation or public interest.

4. Permissions and Invocations

  • Camera: Invoked when you tap “Take Photo,” used to capture photos for stylized images or GIFs (one-time processing).
  • Photo Library Read: Invoked when you choose “Pick from Album,” only processes the photos you select.
  • Photo Library Write: Invoked when you save generated results to the system album.

Permissions are requested just-in-time in the usage context. You can disable them in system settings. Disabling does not affect features that do not rely on those permissions.

5. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

  1. Commissioned processing / necessary sharing (not sold for commercial purposes):
    • Cloud platform: file storage for generated outputs;
    • Payments/receipt verification: Apple (App Store/IAP) — for order verification and delivery.
  2. We only share within what is necessary for functions or compliance, require partners to fulfill security obligations, and will notify you.
  3. Transfer: If personal information is transferred due to merger, division, or reorganization, we will notify you and ensure the recipient remains bound by this policy.
  4. Public disclosure: In principle, we do not publicly disclose personal information; if disclosure is required by law (e.g., by law enforcement), we will disclose minimally and keep records.

6. Data Storage Location and Security

  • Storage location: Before you delete your created results, data is by default stored on servers located in mainland China; once you delete, it will no longer be stored.
  • Security measures: HTTPS/TLS in transit, data minimization and graded management at rest; access controls, auditing, least privilege; backup and disaster recovery.
  • Incident response: If a security incident occurs, we will initiate an emergency plan per regulations and notify you and regulators within a reasonable time.

7. Cross-Border Transfers

We generally do not process data cross-border. If, due to feature updates/upgrades, cross-border processing or storage is truly necessary, we will obtain your separate express consent, perform legally required personal information outbound assessments/standard contractual measures, and inform you of the recipient’s name and contact, processing purposes, methods and types, retention period, and opt-out mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

Within the scope permitted by law, you may exercise the following rights:

  • Access and obtain copies of your generated content;
  • Deletion (e.g., delete items in “My Works”);
  • Account deletion (de-registration);
  • Obtain basic information about our processing (purposes, methods, types, retention periods, sharing, etc.).

We will handle your requests within 15 business days. For security or compliance, we may require identity verification and, where permitted by law, retain response records.

9. Protection of Minors

If you are under 14 years of age, please use the app with your guardian’s explicit consent and guidance. For processing minors’ information, we will adopt stricter protective measures as required by law. Guardians may contact us via “Contact Us” to inquire about, correct, or delete relevant information.

10. Policy Updates

When this policy undergoes material changes (e.g., processing purposes, types, sharing parties, storage locations, or minors’ terms), we will notify you via in-app announcements/pop-ups and, where necessary, obtain your consent again.
